# Project Assignment 

## Project: **ToolShop AI-Ready — Testing the ToolShop**

### Project Name ###

Test The ToolShop 4to6 (**TTT426**)

## 1. Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to plan, manage, coordinate, and deliver the testing activities for the **ToolShop e-commerce platform** as it evolves from its current version toward a market-ready and AI-ready product.

The project focuses on the main objective:

> **Testing the ToolShop to support product quality, market readiness, AI readiness, and release confidence.**

This assignment is defined from a **project management perspective**.

---

## 2. Project Background

ToolShop is an open-source (https://github.com/testsmith-io/practice-software-testing) e-commerce platform that will be further developed in an Agile SDLC. The product will be enhanced in several iterations to support new markets and future AI capabilities.

The testing project is executed as part of a learning journey with students acting in defined roles. The test engineering students are integrated into the Agile product team and collaborate with developers, product ownership, and quality coaching support.

The project is not managed as a separate testing silo. Instead, testing is planned and coordinated as an integrated part of the full product delivery lifecycle.

---

## 3. Project Objective

The main objective of the project is:

> **To manage and execute the testing of ToolShop in a structured, transparent, and value-driven way, ensuring that the product reaches defined quality and readiness goals for each project phase.**

The project shall provide:

- A clear test management structure
- Product-level test planning
- Sprint-level test planning
- Coordination of test activities and their testware
- Risk-based quality reporting
- Release readiness recommendations
- Evidence for decision-making

## 5. Project Scope

### In Scope

The project includes the management and coordination of testing activities (aka Test Project) for ToolShop.

This includes:

- Creation of a product-level test plan
- Creation of sprint-level test plans
- Test activity planning and tracking
- Test progress monitoring
- Defect tracking coordination
- Risk management related to product quality
- Coordination of performance testing
- Coordination of security testing
- Coordination of customer acceptance testing
- Coordination of compliance-related testing activities
- Preparation of test status reports
- Preparation of phase readiness reports
- Final quality and release recommendation

### Out of Scope

The following items are not part of this project assignment:

- Full product development management
- Ownership of the product backlog
- Final business go-to-market decision
- Official ISTQB training or certification delivery
- Formal legal compliance certification
- Production operations after release
- Commercial customer support setup

## 10. Success Criteria

The project is considered successful if:

- A product-level test plan is created and maintained
- Sprint-level test plans are created for all relevant sprints
- Testing activities are visible and coordinated within the Agile team
- Key product risks are identified, tracked, and communicated
- Performance, security, customer acceptance, and compliance testing are planned
- Each phase receives a documented readiness assessment
- Stakeholders receive clear quality information for decision-making
- The final project report provides a transparent recommendation for ToolShop readiness
