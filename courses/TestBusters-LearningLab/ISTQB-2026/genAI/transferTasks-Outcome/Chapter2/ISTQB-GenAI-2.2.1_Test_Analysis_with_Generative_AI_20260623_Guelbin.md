# Outcome
## 1. Selected User Story

### US2350 – Provide Product Articles in Czech Language

#### User Story

As a customer from the Czech Republic, I want to read product information in Czech so that I can understand the products and make informed purchasing decisions.

#### Description

To support the Czech market expansion, the webshop shall provide Czech-language product content for selected articles and product categories.

#### Acceptance Criteria

- **ACC-01:** Product titles for supported articles are displayed in Czech when the customer selects the Czech language.
- **ACC-02:** Product descriptions for supported articles are displayed in Czech when the Czech language is active.
- **ACC-03:** Product specifications and technical details are displayed in Czech for supported products.
- **ACC-04:** Product categories included in the Czech market scope provide Czech-language content for all assigned products.
- **ACC-05:** Customers can switch between Czech and other supported languages without losing product context.
- **ACC-06:** Products without available Czech translations display a defined fallback language according to the webshop configuration.
- **ACC-07:** Czech product content is displayed consistently on product listing pages and product detail pages.
- **ACC-08:** Special Czech characters and language-specific formatting are displayed correctly in all product content fields.
- **ACC-09:** Search results display Czech product titles and descriptions when the webshop language is set to Czech.
- **ACC-10:** Czech-language product content is visible during the complete customer journey, including product detail, cart, and checkout overview pages.

## 3. Review and Refinement of the Generated Test Conditions

The test conditions generated by GenAI were manually reviewed to identify duplicate, similar, or non-verifiable test conditions.

### Review Results

- No non-verifiable test conditions were identified.

- **TC-13** and **TC-14** were merged because both verify the same functionality (localized search results) and refer to the same acceptance criterion (**ACC-09**). The merged test condition verifies that Czech product titles and product descriptions are displayed correctly in the search results.

- **TC-15**, **TC-16**, and **TC-17** were also merged because they all verify the visibility and consistency of Czech-language product content throughout the complete customer journey and are derived from the same acceptance criterion (**ACC-10**). The new test condition covers the product detail page, cart, and checkout overview.

- **TC-07** and **TC-14** (fallback behavior) were reviewed but not merged. Although both are based on **ACC-06**, they verify different aspects:
  - **TC-07** verifies that the configured fallback language is displayed for products without a Czech translation.
  - **TC-14** verifies that products without a Czech translation do not display empty or broken content fields.

- **TC-05** and **TC-06** were not merged because they represent different user scenarios. They verify language switching in opposite directions and therefore remain separate test conditions.

### Summary

The review reduced redundant test conditions while maintaining complete coverage of the acceptance criteria. The final set of test conditions is traceable, verifiable, and provides a solid basis for the subsequent test design activities.

## 5. Top 5 Test Conditions for Immediate Test Design

The following test conditions were selected based on their business criticality, customer impact, and risk for the Czech market launch.

| Priority | Test Condition | Business Justification |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **TC-01** | Product titles are the primary information customers use to identify products. Incorrect localization has a direct business impact. |
| 2 | **TC-04** | Ensures that all products within the Czech market scope provide Czech-language content. Missing translations could affect a large number of products. |
| 3 | **TC-07** | Verifies the fallback mechanism for products without Czech translations. Without a correct fallback, customers may see incomplete or unusable content. |
| 4 | **TC-13** | Verifies that search results display Czech product titles and descriptions correctly. Customers must be able to find products in their selected language. |
| 5 | **TC-14** | Verifies that Czech-language content remains visible throughout the complete customer journey (Product Detail, Cart, Checkout). Language inconsistencies during the purchase process directly affect the customer experience and purchasing confidence. |
