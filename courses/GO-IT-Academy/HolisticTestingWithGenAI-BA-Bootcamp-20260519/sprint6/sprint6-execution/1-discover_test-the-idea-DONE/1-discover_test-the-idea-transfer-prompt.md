# AI Prompt — Market expansion transfer task

## Objective

Produce one **markdown** evidence brief that answers whether a **real** expansion or pivot idea you choose deserves **Go**, **No-go**, or **More discovery**—using **named sources**, not generic industry prose.

## Context

You apply the same discipline as the sprint “discover / test the idea” hands-on (Czech webshop expansion), but for **your** situation: new geography, segment, channel, or partner ecosystem. Wrong bets are expensive; evidence is cheap at this stage.

## Instructions

1. State one **decision question** in a single sentence (testable, quarter-scale).
2. Define **scope** in five bullets: **products** (one narrow family), **≥3 competitors or substitutes**, **≥1 marketplace or discovery surface**, **risks to validate**, **opportunities to explore**.
3. Collect **≥5 signals** of demand, price, logistics, regulation, or trust. Each signal must include: **label**, **what you observed**, **implication**, **source** (URL, or document title + date, or “internal: role/date” without secrets).
4. End with **Go / No-go / More discovery** and **exactly three** bullet reasons; each reason must reference **specific signals** (e.g. “S3”).
5. Optionally add **one short paragraph**: what **new evidence would falsify** your verdict.

## Constraints

- Do **not** write a theory-only essay; every main claim traces to a source or documented observation.
- **Redact** secrets, customer names, and unreleased figures.
- Prefer **depth on a narrow scope** over broad unsupported claims.

## Expected Output

One markdown document with these sections, in order:

1. Decision question (one sentence)  
2. Scope (five bullets as above)  
3. Signals (table or numbered list, ≥5, each with source)  
4. Verdict + three evidence-based bullets  
5. Optional: falsification paragraph  

Suggested filename: `transfer-market-discovery-<short-topic>.md`

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Decision question is one sentence and testable.  
- [ ] Scope includes concrete products, ≥3 named competitors/substitutes, and ≥1 marketplace or discovery surface.  
- [ ] ≥5 signals, each with an identifiable source.  
- [ ] Verdict is exactly **Go**, **No-go**, or **More discovery**, with **three** bullets tied to named signals.  
- [ ] At least one **risk** and one **opportunity** from the scope appear in the signals or verdict.
