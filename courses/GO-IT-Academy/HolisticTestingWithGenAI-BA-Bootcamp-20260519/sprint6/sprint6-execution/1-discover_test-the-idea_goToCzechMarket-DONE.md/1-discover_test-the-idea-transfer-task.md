# Transfer Task: Discover - Test the Idea

## Objective

ToolShop is considering expansion into the Czech market. Before making product, marketing, or investment decisions, the team needs a structured market evaluation based on business evidence.

---

## Background

The ToolShop has a new investor, and the investor want to growth the customer base. The idea is go to new markets in following order: 
1.) Czech Republic
2.) United States (East cost)
3.) China

---

## Reference to Sprint Content

Use publicly available market information, competitor websites, customer reviews, pricing pages, ecommerce trends, and relevant Czech market data.

## Output Format

A file in markdown format

---

## Task Description


- Define the target customer groups in the Czech market for ToolShop.
- Research at least three existing competitors or alternatives in the Czech market.
- Compare competitor positioning, pricing, product offering, and customer experience.
- Identify potential customer needs, pain points, and buying expectations.
- Evaluate legal, language, logistics, payment, and localization considerations.
- Summarize the main opportunities and risks for ToolShop.
- Provide a recommendation: expand, test with a pilot, or do not expand yet.
- Support the recommendation with clear evidence and assumptions.
