The Seal-Team-42 Story
Once upon a time, in the busy world of software startups, there was a small but ambitious company called TechGenius Inc. With a team of bright minds and a 
revolutionary idea, they set out to develop an e-commerce store for the major tools brand " Roy's-Roy's Tools”.


For several months, the team put their heart and soul into the project, working tirelessly day and night to bring the vision to life. With each sprint, they 
made significant progress, overcoming obstacles and refining their product to perfection. The excitement was palpable as they neared the finish line and investors eagerly awaited the launch of their e-commerce store.


But unfortunately, fate had other plans. Just as the team was on the verge of success, tragedy struck. Due to unforeseen financial difficulties, better 
known as “Eaten by the Black Hole,” TechGenius Inc. faced bankruptcy after Sprint 5 was completed. Without warning, the dream shattered and the team disbanded.


Amid the chaos, a glimmer of hope emerged. A glamor investor recognized the project's potential and hired the world-famous turn-around expert 
"The CharmingOrganizer" and a coding seal team from programming institution 42-Vienna to rescue what was left of TechGenius Inc.'s software.


And now a new chapter begins. The new team, called “Seal-Team-42”, will be equipped with a common goal: to conquer new markets with the 
new e-commerce platform “Roy's-Roy's”.


Equipped with nothing other than the GitHub repository, the team will embark 
on a journey full of challenges, collaboration and the promise of success.


And so, with determination in their hearts and Agile principles as their guide, Seal Team 42 will be ready to write history.
