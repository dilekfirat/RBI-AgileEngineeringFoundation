# US1022 - Compliance Ready

## Estimated: 

## User Story:
As a Product Owner,
I want ToolShop to meet applicable legal, regulatory, and internal policy requirements,
so that the product can be operated, audited, and maintained without compliance-related risks.

## Acceptance Croiteria

### P1 - Critical

* [ ] A valid **Privacy Policy (GDPR)** is available and accessible from the application.
* [ ] A valid **Imprint (Legal Notice)** is available and accessible from the application.
* [ ] A **Cookie Policy** is available and accessible from the application.
* [ ] A cookie consent mechanism is available before non-essential cookies are stored.
* [ ] Users can accept, reject, or configure non-essential cookies.
* [ ] Compliance-related information is available and accessible where required.
* [ ] All legal and compliance links navigate to the correct destination.

### P2 - High

* [ ] Legal and compliance links are consistently available from all relevant pages (e.g. footer).
* [ ] All legal documents are up to date and contain meaningful content.
* [ ] External legal links open successfully without broken links.
* [ ] The ToolShop link navigates to the correct ToolShop page.

### P3 - Medium

* [ ] Legal and compliance pages are accessible using keyboard navigation.
* [ ] Legal pages are responsive and usable on desktop and mobile devices.
* [ ] The presentation of legal information is consistent with the application's design.
